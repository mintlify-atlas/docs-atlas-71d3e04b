## What is SocialiCar?

SocialiCar is a modern peer-to-peer car sharing platform that connects vehicle owners who want to earn money from their unused cars with people who need a vehicle for short-term rentals. Our platform makes it easy, safe, and affordable to share cars within your community.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with your email and complete your profile with verification documents
  </Step>
  <Step title="List or Search">
    Car owners can list their vehicles with photos and details. Renters can search by location, dates, and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect and Book">
    Use our chat system to discuss details, then complete your booking with secure payment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share and Review">
    After the rental period, both parties can leave reviews to build trust in the community
  </Step>
</Steps>
