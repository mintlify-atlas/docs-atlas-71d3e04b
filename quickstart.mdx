## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    Visit the SocialiCar homepage at [https://socialicar.wuaze.com](https://socialicar.wuaze.com) and click the **Registrarse** (Register) button in the navigation bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Out Your Information">
    Complete the registration form with your personal details:
    
    **Personal Information:**
    - **Name** - Your first name (letters only)
    - **Surname** - Your last name (letters only)
    - **Date of Birth** - You must be 18 or older
    - **Email** - A valid email address
    - **Phone** - 9-15 digit phone number
    
    **Security:**
    - **Password** - 7-20 characters with at least:
      - 1 uppercase letter
      - 1 lowercase letter
      - 1 number
    - **Confirm Password** - Must match your password
    
    **Identification:**
    - **ID Type** - Select DNI, NIE, or NIF
    - **ID Number** - Your Spanish identification number
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the **Registrarse** button to create your account. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All passwords are securely hashed using BCrypt before storage. Your sensitive information is protected with industry-standard security measures.
</Note>

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After registration, enhance your profile to build trust with the community:

1. **Upload a Profile Photo**
   - Click on your profile icon
   - Select **Editar Perfil** (Edit Profile)
   - Upload a clear photo of yourself
   - Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, JPG, WebP

2. **Verify Your Identity**
   - Upload a photo of your identification document
   - Upload a photo of your driver's license (if listing a car)

<Warning>
  Identity verification helps build trust in the SocialiCar community and may be required before you can list vehicles or make bookings.
</Warning>

## List Your Vehicle

Ready to earn money from your car? Follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Add Vehicle">
    From your profile page, click **Añadir Coche** (Add Car) or navigate to the vehicle management section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Vehicle Details">
    **Basic Information:**
    ```text
    License Plate: 1234ABC (Spanish format)
    Brand: Select from dropdown (powered by NHTSA API)
    Model: Auto-populated based on brand
    Year: Manufacturing year
    Color: Vehicle color
    Fuel Type: Gasolina, Diésel, Eléctrico, or Híbrido
    Transmission: Manual or Automática
    Vehicle Type: Berlina, SUV, Monovolumen, etc.
    ```
    
    **Location:**
    - Full address (5-part format: street, number, city, province, postal code)
    - Parking type: Calle, Garaje, or Aparcamiento
    
    **Pricing:**
    - Daily rate: €15 - €500 per day
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Photos">
    Upload multiple high-quality photos of your vehicle:
    - Exterior views (front, back, sides)
    - Interior shots
    - Any special features
    - Maximum file size: Check system limits
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Features">
    Check all applicable features your vehicle has:
    
    **Driving Assistance:**
    - GPS
    - Parking sensors
    - Backup camera
    - Cruise control
    - 4x4 traction
    
    **Technology:**
    - Bluetooth
    - WiFi
    - Android Auto
    - Apple CarPlay
    
    **Comfort:**
    - Air conditioning
    - Heated seats
    - ISOFIX child seat anchors
    - Accessibility features
    
    **Accessories:**
    - Roof rack
    - Bike carrier
    - Ski rack
    - Tow hitch
    
    **Policies:**
    - Allow pets
    - Allow smoking
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Your Listing">
    Review all information and click **Guardar** (Save) to publish your listing. Your car will appear in search results immediately!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Consider upgrading to a **Plus** (€6.99/month) or **Premium** (€19.99/month) subscription for priority placement in search results and enhanced visibility!
</Tip>

## Rent a Vehicle

Looking for a car to rent? Here's how to find and book:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for Vehicles">
    On the homepage, use the search form to find available cars:
    
    ```html
    Location: Select from Málaga, Granada, Madrid, Valencia, Barcelona
    Start Date: When you need the car
    End Date: When you'll return it
    ```
    
    Click **Buscar** (Search) to see available vehicles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse and Filter">
    **Use the Filter Panel:**
    - Brand and Model
    - Vehicle Type
    - Fuel Type
    - Price Range (€0-€500)
    - Equipment Features (GPS, Bluetooth, A/C, etc.)
    
    **Use Text Search:**
    Search across brand, model, city, postal code, color, description, fuel type, transmission, and more.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Vehicle Details">
    Click on any vehicle card to see:
    - Detailed specifications
    - Owner information
    - Location and parking details
    - All available features
    - Reviews and ratings
    - High-resolution photos
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact the Owner">
    Click the **Chat** button to:
    - Ask questions about the vehicle
    - Discuss pickup/dropoff details
    - Negotiate pricing for longer rentals
    - Confirm availability
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Booking">
    Once you've agreed with the owner:
    
    1. Confirm the rental dates
    2. Click **Reserve** or **Book Now**
    3. Complete payment through PayPal
    4. Receive booking confirmation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick Up Your Vehicle">
    - Meet the owner at the agreed location
    - Inspect the vehicle together
    - Document any existing damage
    - Receive keys and any instructions
    - Start your trip!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment & Pricing

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Renters">
    **Payment Process:**
    - All payments are processed securely through PayPal
    - You'll be charged for the number of days you book
    - Calculation: Daily Rate × Number of Days
    
    **Example:**
    ```text
    Daily Rate: €45/day
    Rental Period: 3 days
    Total Cost: €45 × 3 = €135
    ```
    
    **What's Included:**
    - Vehicle rental for specified dates
    - Basic insurance (check with owner)
    - Owner support during rental
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="For Owners">
    **Setting Your Price:**
    - Minimum: €15/day
    - Maximum: €500/day
    - Consider: Vehicle type, features, location, demand
    
    **Pricing Tips:**
    - Research similar vehicles in your area
    - Offer weekly/monthly discounts
    - Adjust for peak seasons
    - Premium members get better visibility
    
    **Receiving Payments:**
    - Payments processed through PayPal
    - Funds transferred after booking confirmation
    - Platform fee: (Check current rates)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
